Are you wondering what the weather in Illinois is?
Well, here is it's climate.

The climate in this state is pretty average.
It has warm summers and cold winters.
The average winter temp is about 28 degrees Fahrenheit, while the summer's is 73.5 deggrees Fahrenheit.

The warmest day in this Midwest state was July 14, 1954, In east St.Louis. It was 117 degrees Fahrenheit!
The coldest day held a time with -36 degrees Fahrenheit! It was on January 15 , 1999, in Congerville.
There is about 26 inches of snow a year, and 40 inches of rain.
Each year, there is a average of 50 days of storm. There are about 60 tornadoes each year!
