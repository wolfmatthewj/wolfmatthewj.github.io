Slleepband is a wristband used to sleep. It has many uses, all completely helpful to your sleep. With it you'll never have trouble at night ever again. Here are all it's uses in a nutshell:
  1. It makes you fall asleep slowly and easily
  2. It makes you wake up slowly, and at a chosen time
  3. If you don't get up when your "alarm" goes off, it will pinch until you do
  4. It gives you good dreams
  5. It makes you relax and sleep comfortably

All these uses are extremely helpful, and with Slleepband, you will never have probloms at night again.
