Slleepband's uses are plentiful and helpful. Here are all the uses:

It makes you fall asleep

Slleepband uses a specific frequency to make you fall asllep. Don't worry, this frequency is perfectly safe. It doesn't make you fall asleep instantly, though. This way, your thought prosses won't suddenly be interupted.

It makes you slowly wake up

Slleepband also makes you wake up at a set time. It's like an alarm clock, but better. Alarm clocks aren't guaranteed to wake you up, but Slleepband is. This wristband uses a combanation of soft light, sound, and other means to slowly make your eyes open at the right time. To make sure you aren't late, the band will pinch you until you get up. This feature can be turned off.

It gives you good dreams

Slleepband can also give you good, more vivid dreams. It works becase a very specific type of brain activity in the frontal lobes, called gamma activity, is present during lucid dreams. Lucid dreams are dreams where it actually feels like you're conscious. Slleepband makes uses of the brain activity to control your dreams.

It makes you relax and sleep comfortably

One of Slleepband's smaller uses is that it makes you relax. It uses light, calming music and waves to make you do no more rolling around.

And there you have it. All of Slleepband's uses to make you have a great rest.
