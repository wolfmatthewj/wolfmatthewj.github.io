  • The Illinois State Fair


    The Illinois State Fair is one of the many fun and great state fairs. It happens every august annually, in Springfield. The time it was created was 1853. At the beginning, it was mainly just Farmers comparing notes. It moved from 1853-92. By 1894, it was in Springfield. Then in 1895, a Dome building was bought for $69,000 from Chicago. The diameter was 222 feet! It held 10,000. But there was a fire in 1917, and it collapsed.

    There was no fair from 1942-45, due to a Air force supply depot being held on the grounds. After world war one, there came more and more entertanment. After 2012, the attendance droped. In 2013 it was 961,143. But in 2018,it was 369,144. This reversed in 2019, haveing 509,000 people.

    Now there is the count is 100 just for buildings! That isn't counting the tons of pavillians and trailers. The Illinois state fair is an excellent place to go for fun and excitement.
