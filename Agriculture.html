1. Crops

  • Corn

    Corn is a very big part of Illinois's agriculture. Illinois is the U.S's 2nd producer of corn.They produse 56,280 militers of corn a year! All of this is use to make Tortillas, Popcorn, Corn Bread, Sweetened milk, Cooking oil, and corn starch. it also can be eaten on the cob, or as Sweetcorn. Leftovers can be used to feed livestock.

  • Wheat

    Illinois makes 1,696,288,000 liters of wheat. It can make bread, biscuts, crackers, scones, pancaces, and pitza.

  • Soybeans

    Illinois is the top produser of soybeans. They make 22,528,000 liters of it! They have a lot of protein. They are used to make oil, milk, flour, and tofu. You can also just eat them.

2. Cattle (Animals)

  • Hogs

    There are a great 11 million hogs in Illinois. All these can be eaten in the form of Pork, bacon, or a hotdog. Some people even keep them as pets!

    There is 1,380,000 cows in Illinois. Their milk is used for dariy producs, the meat for eating, and the skin for lether.

  • horses

    These 200,000 horses can be fun. People ride and have races on them. They also were used for transportation before cars.

  • Sheep

    What's not to like about these 55,000 sheep. Their made use is to get wool. You can also eat it's meat. They even make make milk, like cows!

3. Other
